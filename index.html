Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for weddings and events during peak season (May-October). However, we often accommodate last-minute bookings when availability permits.

What is included in the packages?

All packages include professional photography coverage, edited high-resolution images, online gallery access, and print release. Specific inclusions vary by package - please refer to the package details for exact coverage hours and deliverables.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes! We travel throughout the region. Travel fees apply for locations beyond 50 miles from our studio. Destination weddings and events are welcome - contact us for custom travel packages.

How long until I receive my photos?

Standard delivery is 2-3 weeks for portrait and event sessions, and 4-6 weeks for weddings. Rush editing is available for an additional fee. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours of your session.

Can I request specific shots or poses?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share inspiration photos, shot lists, or specific moments you want captured. During our consultation, we'll discuss your vision and create a plan to ensure all your must-have shots are included.

What if weather affects an outdoor session?

We monitor weather closely and will contact you 24-48 hours before outdoor sessions if conditions look unfavorable. We can reschedule at no additional cost or move to an indoor location. Your comfort and safety are our priority.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all packages. A 50% deposit secures your date, with the remaining balance due on or before the session date. We accept cash, check, credit cards, and bank transfers.

Can I purchase additional prints or albums?

Yes! We offer professional printing services, custom albums, canvas prints, and wall art. All products are printed through professional labs ensuring museum-quality results. Pricing available upon request.
